This is a rare opportunity to own a private 3-acre estate with a custom home, attached garage, pond, year-round creek, and a sizable, detached shop/garage. This exceptional property includes three separate 1-acre tax lots with flexible R-1-6 zoning, offering significant development potential. Bring your developer and envision the possibilities. The 3,951 sq ft home features vaulted ceilings, tile countertops, a Sub-Zero refrigerator and custom oak cabinetry with pull-outs. The primary suite includes a mini-split, soaking tub, walk-in shower, dual vanities, and French doors opening to a private deck. An attached two-car garage complements the detached three-car garage with full bath and hobby space. Landscaped grounds feature a heated in-ground pool, poolside changing room, built-in gas fire pit, water rights, and gated side entry. Set in a peaceful, park-like setting, this one-of-a-kind property offers privacy, space, and future potential in a truly beautiful environment.
